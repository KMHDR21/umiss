ABOUT UM




     Universitas Negeri Malang is one of the reputable universities in Indonesia. Consisting of eight faculties with a total of 120 highly ranked study programs, UM has succeeded in delivering its students to become bachelors, masters, and doctorates who are ready to face the global world. As one of the top 20 universities in Indonesia, UM continuously tries to provide excellent education and resources for the development of science, research, and technology in Indonesia. Located in Malang city, the second-biggest city in East Java and is well known as a city of education, UM offers a great atmosphere of various academic and cultural activities for students to pursue their degree. More information can be found on the official website of Universitas Negeri Malang: www.um.ac.id

UM ISS

Universitas Negeri Malang International Student Scholarship (UM-ISS) is a prestigious scholarship offered by Universitas Negeri Malang to provide access for highly qualified international students around the world. This scholarship program provides a free Indonesian language course for 5 months and free tuition for selected-eligible international students to pursue their bachelor’s, master’s, or doctoral degree in Universitas Negeri Malang, Indonesia. It is disbursed for a maximum period of 5 years for Undergraduate, 3 for Master’s, and 4 for Doctoral (inclusive of language program), on condition that the scholarship grantee maintains a record of good academic performance and conduct compliant with university codes. This program facilitates scholars and students from around the world to experience not only the dynamic of higher education in Indonesia, but also the vibes of Indonesian cultural diversities. UM-ISS also offers an exclusive experience of expanding knowledge about various cultures while studying in Malang city.

Objectives :

  • Contributing to the development of human resources in academic sectors;
  • Promoting cultural exchange and cross-cultural understanding;
  • Giving equal opportunities for the students from developing countries and developed countries to pursue higher-education in Indonesia;



Scholarship Coverage :

    The UM-ISS will cover awardee’s:
  • Indonesian Language Training at Universitas Negeri Malang (max. 5 months);
  • Dormitory fees;
  • Tuition fees in any study programs. The maximum durations are:
    • - Bachelor degree (max. 5 years)
    - Master degree (max. 3 years)
    - Doctorate degree (max. 4 years)

Bachelor Master Doctoral

ELIGIBILITY

  • Maximum age for application: 30 years old.
  • Applicants must be Non-Indonesian Citizens.
  • Be in good health.
  • Have a good command of English and or Indonesian.
  • Have graduated from high school.
  • Possess excellent co-curricular records showing strong leadership potential and/or qualities.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • A scan copy of previous degree certificate(s) and transcript(s) (High School). If not in English, please provide the certified English translation (PDF);
  • A scan copy of a Passport-Size photo (JPG/ PNG);
  • A scan copy of passport (PDF);
  • A scan copy of an English Proficiency test result (TOEFL or IELTS) (PDF);
  • Health Affidavit (PDF);
  • A Letter of Declaration;
  • A Letter of Financial Guarantee Statement;
  • Two Letters of Recommendation from school teachers (PDF – two letters in one PDF);
    • Note: The language proficiency certificate must be obtained within the last 2 years.

    Step 1: UM-ISS Online Registration
  • Visit the UM ISS website.;
  • Create an account and fill in the required data, including name, email, and passwords;
  • Check your email to activate your account;
  • Fill in the UM-ISS online application;
  • Upload all the required documents;
  • After you have provided all the information and documents, submit your application;

    • Step 2: Selection and Announcement
  • Selection process: 3 - 21 May 2021;
