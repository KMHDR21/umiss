How to Apply

ELIGIBILITY

  • Maximum age for application: 30 years old for undergraduate, 35 years old for master, and 37 years old for doctorate;
  • Do not hold Indonesian citizenship;
  • Possess prerequisite educational qualification(s) deemed appropriate to undertake the intended program at UM;
  • Possess excellent co-curricular records showing strong leadership potential and/or qualities;

REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT

  • A scan copy of previous degree certificates and transcripts. If not in English, please provide the certified English translation (PDF).
  • A scan copy Passport Size photo (JPG/ PNG).
  • A scan copy of passport (PDF).
  • A scan copy of English Proficiency test result (TOEFL or IELTS) (PDF).
  • Health Affidavit (PDF). Download the format here
  • A completed Letter of Declaration. Download the format here
  • A completed Letter of Financial Guarantee Statement. Download the format here
  • Two Letters of Recommendation from school teachers or lecturers for postgraduate applicants (PDF â€“ two letters in one PDF).

HOW TO APPLY

  • Choose a degree and study program, visit here to see the offered study programs;
  • Prepare all the required documents;
  • Open UM ISS application form bit.ly/UMISS_2020;
  • Complete all the personal information in the application form;
  • After you have completed all the steps, check all the information and documents you have provided;
  • Click submit to finish your application.
